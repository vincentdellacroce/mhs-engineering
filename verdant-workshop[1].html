§ 02 — How I Work
§ Design

I design from the house out.

Every garden I make begins with the building it belongs to. The sightlines from inside, the architecture of the façade, the materials already in the ground. The garden should feel like it grew alongside the house, not after it.

§ Build

Slow work.
Four projects a year.

I work with two builders, both from the Hudson Valley, both with more experience in dry-stone construction than I'll ever have. We take on four projects each year. No more. The work takes as long as it takes.

§ Grow

The garden you didn't
know you wanted.

I check in at one year and three years after installation. Something always needs adjusting. The plants that thrived, the ones that didn't — that information shapes what I plant next. It's a slow education.
